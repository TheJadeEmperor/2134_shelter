Initial Protests

Hundreds of community members and local politicians, protested the city's decision to replace the planned affordable housing with a homeless shelter. The biggest supporters are Dimple Willabus, candidate for City Council D46, as well as Curtis Sliwa, candidate for Mayor. Source

Residents and local leaders expressed opposition to the proposed shelter, citing concerns about safety. Councilmember Inna Vernikov criticized the change as a "bait and switch." Around this time, an online petition appeared to oppose the shelter. Link
